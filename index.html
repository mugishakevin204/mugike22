Welcome to the official dashboard of the Past Paper Exam Website, your trusted online platform designed to make exam preparation smarter and easier. Our mission is to support students, teachers, and schools across Rwanda by providing quick and organized access to past national examination papers for Primary 6 (P6), Senior 3 (S3), and Senior 6 (S6). Through this digital platform, learners can browse, preview, and download authentic past exam papers to help them revise efficiently and understand the real format of national exams. Education is the foundation of success, and our goal is to make quality study materials accessible to every learner regardless of location or school resources. By offering past papers in one place, we aim to save students valuable time and encourage consistent practice, which is the key to academic excellence. Teachers and parents can also use this system to guide learners through structured revision sessions, track progress, and identify areas for improvement. This platform continues to grow, and soon we will add more features such as automatic marking, discussion forums, and access to helpful study tips. Together, we believe that with the right tools and dedication, every student can achieve outstanding results.
